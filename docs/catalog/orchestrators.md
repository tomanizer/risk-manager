# Orchestrator Catalog

## Daily Risk Investigation
Daily workflow for selecting, routing, synthesizing, challenging, and handing off material daily targets.

## Limit Breach
Workflow for validating, explaining, escalating, and routing material breach cases.

## PLA Deterioration
Workflow for handling desk-level regulatory-control deterioration.

## Month-End Review
Workflow for separating expected period-end effects from abnormal cases.

## Desk Status / Capital Impact
Workflow for translating signals into desk posture, watchlist state, and consequence views.

## Model / Change Impact
Workflow for assessing the impact of releases, methodology changes, and related scope changes.

## Governance Pack
Workflow for curating periodic management and committee outputs.
