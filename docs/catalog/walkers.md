# Walker Catalog

## Quant Walker
Interprets structural analytical drivers.

## Time Series Walker
Interprets persistence, outliers, recurrence, and regime behavior.

## Data Controller Walker
Interprets trust, readiness, and false-signal conditions.

## Controls / Change Walker
Interprets internal process, release, valuation, and change causality.

## Market Context Walker
Interprets whether external market behavior supports the internal story.

## Governance / Reporting Walker
Turns findings into management-ready narrative blocks.

## Critic / Challenge Walker
Applies adversarial review before governance handoff.

## Presentation / Visualization Walker
Chooses best human-readable form and layout for outputs.

## Model Risk & Usage Walker
Interprets intended use, limitations, boundaries, and open issues for relevant models.
